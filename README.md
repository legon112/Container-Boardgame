A round consists of 4 stages:

### 1. Container Inspection 
* Teams read the container's backstory and view the **visible** contents.
* Other items may be hidden behind the visible ones; the full contents remain unknown until purchase.
* Teams can talk openly and react to the contents during this phase.

### 2. Tactics Discussion 
* Teams discuss their strategy privately (e.g., setting a price limit, driving up the price for opponents, or sitting out).
* **Psychological Warfare:** Bluffing is encouraged.

### 3. Auction 
* The moderator announces the **Starting Price** and the **Step** (minimum increment).
* Teams can bid the starting price or raise it by at least one Step.
* Bids are accepted immediately if they meet the criteria.
* The moderator counts down from 1 to 3. After "3", the moderator must say: **"Sold!"**.

### 4. Review and Calculation 
* The winning bid amount is deducted from the team's **Budget**.
* The team's container count increases by 1.
* The moderator "sells" the items. The total value of the items is added to the team's **PROFIT** (not the budget).
